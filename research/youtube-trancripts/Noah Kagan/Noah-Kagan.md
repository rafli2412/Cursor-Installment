## Best use for B2B SaaS teams

From these videos, the most practical YouTube strategy pattern is:

1. Build around a specific audience and problem, not generic founder content.
2. Publish high-quality videos consistently before scaling promotion.
3. Use email, collaborations, and partnerships to distribute the videos.
4. Test paid promotion only after the funnel has a clear ROI path.
5. Connect YouTube to a business outcome: leads, newsletter subscribers, trials, demos, or product education.
