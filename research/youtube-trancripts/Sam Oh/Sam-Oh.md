## 1. How to use Video Marketing to Grow Your Business

**Type:** Video  
**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QCgDIhvFkCM  
**Why it was selected:** This is the broadest and most business-oriented pick. It helps frame YouTube as more than a publishing platform: for B2B SaaS, it can support awareness, education, trust-building, and eventual product demand.

**B2B SaaS application:**

- Use YouTube to educate prospects before they enter a sales conversation.
- Build trust by teaching real problems your audience is already trying to solve.
- Prioritize videos that connect audience pain points to product-adjacent expertise.
- Treat video as a long-term acquisition asset, not just a campaign format.

**Best use case:** SaaS founders, growth teams, and content marketers who need a strategic starting point for why video matters and how it can support business growth.

## 2. How I'd Use YouTube SEO to Get Millions of Views if I Had to Start Over

**Type:** Video  
**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lgUrHqaPrhU  
**Why it was selected:** This is one of the most directly relevant Ahrefs videos for modern YouTube strategy. It focuses on using YouTube SEO to build compounding discovery, which is especially useful for B2B SaaS companies in non-viral or “boring” categories.

**B2B SaaS application:**

- Start with search demand instead of random content ideas.
- Build videos around problems, jobs-to-be-done, and high-intent queries.
- Optimize for both YouTube search and Google video results where possible.
- Use educational content to attract audiences who are already problem-aware.

**Best use case:** SaaS teams creating evergreen YouTube content around product categories, workflows, tutorials, comparisons, and pain-point searches.

## 3. YouTube Keyword Research: How to Get More Views Consistently

**Type:** Video  
**URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kAuM2nQb_P8  
**Why it was selected:** Keyword research is the foundation of a repeatable B2B SaaS YouTube content system. This video is useful for choosing topics that people are actively searching for, instead of relying only on internal assumptions or founder opinions.

**B2B SaaS application:**

- Build a video topic backlog from real search behavior.
- Identify problem-aware and solution-aware keywords.
- Prioritize topics that align with buying intent, onboarding needs, and category education.
- Use keyword research to reduce wasted production effort on low-demand topics.

**Best use case:** Content teams building a YouTube editorial calendar for SaaS categories such as CRM, SEO tools, analytics, project management, automation, AI tools, or cybersecurity.

---

## Key Takeaways for B2B SaaS Teams

1. **Search-first YouTube works well for B2B SaaS.** SaaS topics often have steady informational demand, even when they are not naturally viral.
2. **Product-adjacent education is the best angle.** The strongest SaaS videos teach the audience how to solve a problem while naturally positioning the product category.
3. **Keyword research should guide the content calendar.** Ahrefs’ approach is useful because it turns YouTube ideation into a repeatable process.
4. **Evergreen tutorials can compound over time.** Unlike social posts, YouTube videos can keep attracting new viewers through search long after publishing.
5. **The strategy is most useful for B2B SaaS teams in complex or educational categories.** Examples include SEO, analytics, AI, martech, sales software, customer support, finance software, and developer tools.

---

## Final Recommendation

For B2B SaaS content strategy research, Ahrefs is one of the strongest channels to study. The best lesson from this channel is not just individual video tactics, but the broader operating model: use YouTube like a search-driven education engine that builds trust, captures demand, and supports product-led growth.
