## How to Implement Owen Video's B2B SaaS YouTube Strategy

### Phase 1: Foundation (Month 1-2)
1. Define your revenue strategy (not just vanity metrics)
2. Identify 24 industry questions your B2B customers ask
3. Plan your long-form content pillars (4-6 core topics)
4. Set up your YouTube channel with clear messaging

### Phase 2: Content Production (Month 2+)
1. **Weekly:** 20-minute planning session
2. **Monthly:** Batch record 1-2 long-form videos (8-12 minutes each)
3. **Weekly:** Repurpose each long-form into 4-6 Shorts (30-60 sec each)
4. **Weekly:** Create 3-5 short-form vertical videos from your long-form content
5. Distribute shorts across YouTube, LinkedIn, TikTok, Instagram Reels

### Phase 3: Optimization (Ongoing)
1. Track leads generated (not just views)
2. Monitor conversion from short-form to long-form
3. Refine based on what generates revenue
4. Scale what works, double down on high-conversion content
